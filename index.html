HIT

This is a decision that a player makes to order for another card from the dealer
to make his hand a good hand by having a hand value of 17 or higher.
However if the dealer has a face-up card of 7 or higher the player should aim for a hand value
of 18 or higher

WHEN TO HIT

You should hit in black jack when your hand value is 11 or lower or when the dealers
face-up card is 7 or higher and your hand value is 12-16.(Hand signal- Tap the felt with your finger)

STAND

If your fisrt two cards are accepatable or with good hand value, you can stand
and the dealer will move to the next player.[Hand-signal: Wave your hand or simply put out
open palm over the felt.]

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Place your bet: Before the cards are dealt, players must place a bet. The minimum and maximum bets are usually
    posted on the table.
  2. Receive your cards: Once all bets have been placed, the dealer will deal two cards to each player, face up.
  3. Decide to hit or stand: After receiving your two cards, you can choose to “hit” and receive additional cards
    or “stand” and keep your current hand
  4. Dealer’s turn: After all players have had their turn, the dealer will reveal their face-down card and hit or
    stand according to predetermined rules.
  5. Determine the winner: If neither the player nor the dealer busts, the person with the highest hand value wins.
